My Story

My path in software developing started on my third year studies at the Oulu Business School. I was struggling with my statistical programming classes and I wanted to get better. So, I enrolled to a Python course where I learned the very basics of programming. From the very first steps of printing ”hello world” to the console, I was immediately hooked on programming.

During my studies, I got more and more interested about programming than my studies at the business school. After graduation, I had an empty feeling about my future career: should I continue my previous path in sales or should I continue my journey in programming. It was then when I resigned from my sales agent job when I found the miracle of web-development.

My previous experience in programming was limited to only data-analysis and scientific computing. After founding the joy of web development, I enrolled to a front-end development course on Codecademy and started getting a grasp of modern web development. Only in a matter of month, I was already acing the web development fundamentals and my skills got better day by day. At the moment, I am at the point where nothing is impossible and I am eager to gain more experience in the world of modern web development.
